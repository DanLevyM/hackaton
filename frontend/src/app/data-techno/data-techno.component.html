SIMILARITIES WITH MEDICAMENT PROTOCOLS

Skin care product testing often resembles the protocols of medicament testing. It is a long sequential process which aims to protect the voluntary testers, measure performance, record side-effects. For the most serious cosmetic labs, it is not rare to spend over 18 months gathering and analyzing the results of the following stages.

1
Scientific literature analysis Molecule Dosage Application Frequency
2
Trials on reconstructed skin or explants or animals
3
Clinical trials on healthy and affected testers
4
Double bind test on large basis vs. placebo vs. existing formula
5
Surveillance
